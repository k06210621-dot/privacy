PredictX Sports Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-29

Thank you for using PredictX Sports (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. Please read the following carefully before use.

1. Information We Collect

The App is an informational service that does not accept wagers or sell prediction products. We collect the following data:

  • APNs push identifier: Device token generated by the system when you enable push notifications (used to send high-confidence game alerts; you can disable push at any time within the App)
  • Subscription data: Subscription tier (Free / Basic / Standard / Premium) and transaction history processed through Apple StoreKit (payment data is handled by Apple; the App does not store credit card information directly)
  • Prediction viewing history: AI predictions you have viewed (used for personalized accuracy statistics)
  • Device preferences: Favorited teams, dark mode preference, unlocked games (stored locally on your device only, not uploaded to the server)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA): Used by Google AdMob for personalized ads only with your consent (you can disable at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking)

Note: The App does not collect your email, name, or Apple ID personal identifier, and has no sign-up or login mechanism. iOS version and device model are also not in the scope of data collection.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver daily sports (MLB / NPB / CPBL / NBA) AI prediction content
  • Manage 3-tier subscription (Basic / Standard / Premium, including a Free plan) and restore-purchase functionality
  • Display personalized prediction history and accuracy rates
  • Serve Google AdMob ads (you can opt out via system settings)
  • Improve app quality and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: processes subscription payments
  • Google AdMob: serves ads (including Google User Messaging Platform for consent)
  • Cloud backend API: provides game data and AI predictions (hosted at predictx-sports-production.up.railway.app)

These services have their own privacy policies; the App is not responsible for their content.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is transmitted via HTTPS encryption. The backend uses PostgreSQL to store APNs device tokens, subscription tiers, and prediction viewing history. Device-side preferences (favorites, dark mode, unlocked games) are stored in iOS UserDefaults and exist only on your local device. Ads served by Google AdMob may involve cross-device tracking, which you may disable at any time via iOS Settings.

5. Data Retention

  • APNs push token: retained until you uninstall the App or manually disable push
  • Prediction viewing history: retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Device-side preferences: can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal data
  • Cancel subscription (iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions)
  • Disable personalized ads (iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking)

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have done so, please contact us for deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
